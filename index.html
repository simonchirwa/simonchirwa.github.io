How to Use

  1. Login or create an account with your email, username, and password.
  2. Navigate through Home, Service, Timer, Settings, and How to Use sections.
  3. Use the Timer section to set studying reminders, timetable reminders, or your own custom reminders.
  4. Upload documents or study materials if needed.
  5. When a reminder triggers, the screen will show a full alarm with message: "Time to Study" for at least 30 minutes until dismissed.
  6. Customize your account settings, toggle dark mode, or update your password.
